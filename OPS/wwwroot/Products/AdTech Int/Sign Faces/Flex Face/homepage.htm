Digitally printed flex face & cabinet.		  

Flexible-face signage is a much safer alternative to rigid faces, especially in regions with high winds and storms. If there’s a sign blow out, rigid materials can shatter and become flying projectiles.

Flex is also appropriate for signs with faces larger than 10 feet by 10 feet because making these signs with rigid faces would require a seam in the polycarbonate that many customers find unattractive. Flexible-faces should not be used for signs that are low to the ground, like monument signs as the flex face material is susceptible to vandalism.

Flex face can be decorated with digital printing or applied vinyl. When ordering be sure to the specify copy area and trim size that you require.
