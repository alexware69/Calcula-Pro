Modified Acrylic
Acrylic possesses the rigidity essential for signage use. Acrylic's uniform composition and glass-like sheen are well-suited for light transmission and color rendition. Acrylic resists fading and general discoloration. It is also known for the fact that clear is clear and white is white as compared to polycarbonate. If your application requires thermoforming vinyl to the sign face, it can only be done using acrylic.
