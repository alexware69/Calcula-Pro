Painted faces are clear plastic with the paint applied to the second surface (side next to the lamps). These can use any molding option.

When choosing your paint colors be sure that you are not using all opaque colors. PMS (Pantone) colors with one part black or less will be translucent.

In this sample PMS #483 will be opaque with 4pts (part) of black and PMS #484 will be translucent.
