Decorating with Digital Printing

If your application calls for multiple colors and gradients that touch then Digital printing will be the most cost effective solution. Digitally printed faces will also have the least amount of fabrication lead time and are also very cost effective for multiple face requirements. Digital printing is the only way to reproduce photographic images, but the range of colors is limited to those that can be produced using CMYK inks.

Typical applications are:

First surface on a white flat or panned face.

2nd surface on a clear face; back sprayed white. This is a good option when the face may be subject to vandalism.

2nd surface on a clear face; pan & flange painted a color, back sprayed white.

NOTE: All printed colors are translucent except 4 color black.
