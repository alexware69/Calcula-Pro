In panned & debossed faces the text and/or shapes are below the surface of the face instead of raised above the face. The debossed depth is determined by the stroke and size of the letter or shape. Debossed areas are cut with a 7 degree draft to allow the plastic to release from the mold.

General debossed copy depths:
4" to 5" are debossed ¼ inch.
5” or taller will be debossed ½ inch.

NOTE: Letters under 4” cannot be debossed.
Letter strokes less than ½ inch wide cannot be debossed.
