Pan embossed faces are similar to pan faces, but some or all of the copy or shapes may be raised. Dies are placed on top of the pan mold to create this effect. Embossed pans can cosmetically improve the appearance of the face as well as add additional strength. The embossing height is determined by the stroke and size of the letter or shape. The dies are cut with a 7 degree draft to allow the plastic to release from the mold.

General embossed copy heights:
4" to 5" are embossed ¼ inch.
5” or taller will be embossed ½ inch.

NOTE: Letters under 4” cannot be embossed.
Letter strokes less than ½ inch wide cannot be embossed.
