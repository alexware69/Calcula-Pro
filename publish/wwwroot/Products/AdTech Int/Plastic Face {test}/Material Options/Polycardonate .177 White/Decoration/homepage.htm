Sign Face Decorating

There are numerous options for decorating the sign face. The three primary methods for decorating a face are Paint, Applied Vinyl or Digital Printing.

A typical sign has a background color and a copy color. The background or copy can be either translucent (light will pass through) or opaque (light will not pass through). Opaque colors will appear black when back lighting is used.

The simplest and most cost effective face that can be used is made with white acrylic or polycarbonate sign face material with vinyl or digital print applied to the first surface.

A combination of two or more decorating methods can be used to decorate a face. Obviously the more colors, methods and techniques that are required to decorate a face the more complex they are and ultimately more expensive.
