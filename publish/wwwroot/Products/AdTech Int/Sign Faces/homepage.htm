Sign Face Material Options

There are a number of options available when deciding on the type of sign face material for your project.

They are:

  • Aluminum
  • Flex Face
  • Plastic sign faces (acrylic or polycarbonate)

Face options include rigid flat or formed, embossing, debossing, custom molds, routed aluminum, and flexible to accommodate most any application..

Replacement or undecorated options can also be ordered.

Click on the box next to Sign Faces to choose your face material.
